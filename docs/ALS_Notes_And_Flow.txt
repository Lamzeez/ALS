I want to make this system where Schools/ALS Centers can register their school/center on our site, where the System Admin (developers/us for now) will have to review the school's registration request and once verified, the newly registered ALS center will be displayed on the "ALS Center" dropdown menu item from the registration page from the app (but it's currently named as "student_phone" folder even though both students and teachers will be using it. Once a school's registered, the school admin will then be able to list the category of core academic subjects that their school can teach students like these courses, programs, and subjects in @docs/CoreAcademicSubjects.pdf.
For the teachers to register in their school as a teacher, they will register through the "student_phone" app, select the "Teacher" toggle, provide necessary information, and after clicking "Create Account", the teacher will wait for the School Admin or the ALS Center Admin's approval. Once approved, the teacher can now create specific courses within a core academic subject, structure his/her course with an organized timeline, and upload learning materials that the students can view or download offline in which they can view later in the app when they don't have internet connection.
For the students to register, they will register to the same student_phone app by toggling the "Student" option and providing the necessary information. Once registered, they will have to enroll in a course that the teacher created by scanning the QR code of the course or by entering the course PIN/code that the teacher has provided them. Once they're in, they will be able to see all the learning materials that the teacher has uploaded, as well as the timeline for the course, as well as their course's completion progress which they'll be seeing live. Once the course is complete, the students will then have a short congratulatory virtual certificate.
